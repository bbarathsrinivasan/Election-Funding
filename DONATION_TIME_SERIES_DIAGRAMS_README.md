# Donation Time Series Diagrams

## Scenario Summary Table

| Scenario | What it shows | Calculation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Democratic cumulative ratio | Relative scale of Democratic donations over time (all donors plus Small/Medium/Large segments) | $\frac{\text{Dem} + \text{Rep}}{\text{Dem}}$ |
| Republican cumulative ratio | Relative scale of Republican donations over time (all donors plus Small/Medium/Large segments) | $\frac{\text{Dem} + \text{Rep}}{\text{Rep}}$ |
| Democratic donations | Total Democratic donations per period (all donors plus Small/Medium/Large segments) | $\sum \text{Dem donations in period}$ |
| Republican donations | Total Republican donations per period (all donors plus Small/Medium/Large segments) | $\sum \text{Rep donations in period}$ |
